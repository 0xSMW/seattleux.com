# Portfolio review checklist

## Story and framing

- [ ] Clear role and scope
- [ ] Problem statement and constraints
- [ ] What “success” meant

## Process and decisions

- [ ] Key decisions and tradeoffs explained
- [ ] Evidence used (research, data, stakeholder input)
- [ ] Collaboration and influence described

## Outcomes

- [ ] What shipped (or what changed)
- [ ] Impact measures (qualitative or quantitative)
- [ ] Learnings and what you’d do differently

## Craft

- [ ] Easy to scan
- [ ] Accessible contrast and readable text
- [ ] Links work
