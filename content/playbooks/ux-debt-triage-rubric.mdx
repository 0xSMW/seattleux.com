# UX debt triage rubric

## Score each item (1–5)

- User impact (severity)
- Frequency (how often it occurs)
- Business impact
- Accessibility risk
- Support load impact
- Implementation effort (invert score if you prefer)

## Decision rule

Prioritize items with:

- high impact × high frequency
- high legal/compliance or accessibility risk
- strong linkage to key funnels or retention

## Output

- Ranked list
- Clear “why now”
- Owners and timeline
