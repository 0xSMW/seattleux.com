# Product discovery workshop agenda (90 minutes)

## Prep (async)

- Share brief, constraints, and any existing evidence
- Define the decision you want to make

## Agenda

1. Goal + decision statement (5 min)
2. Evidence review (15 min)
3. Problem framing (15 min)
4. Opportunity generation (15 min)
5. Evaluate by criteria (20 min)
6. Decision + owners (10 min)
7. Risks + next steps (10 min)

## Outputs

- Top opportunity statement
- Next experiment / prototype plan
- Owners and dates
